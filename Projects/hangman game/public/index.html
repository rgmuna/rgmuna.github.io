• To start a new game, follow the instructions in the message center in the middle of the page. When two people have signed on, the game will begin.
  • Blank spaces representing the answer word will appear on the page.
  • Use the letter buttons at the bottom of the page to guess what letters are part of the answer word.
  • If you guess a letter correctly, the letter will show up in one of the blank spaces and your color progress bar will raise.
  • If you answer incorrectly, your HANGMAN will start to be drawn.
  • Keep an eye on your opponent's progress bar because if they figure out the word correctly first, they will win the game.
  • The first person to guess the word correctly or the last person standing wins!
  • If it seems like the page has crashed, press the "reset" button in the upper left corner.
