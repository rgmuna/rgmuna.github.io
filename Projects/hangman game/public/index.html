Instructions

  1. To start a new game, follow the instructions in the message center in the middle of the page. When two people have signed on, the game will begin.
  2. Blank spaces representing the answer word will appear on the page.
  3. Use the letter buttons at the bottom of the page to guess what letters are part of the answer word.
  4. If you guess a letter correctly, the letter will show up in one of the blank spaces and your color progress bar will raise.
  5. If you answer incorrectly, your HANGMAN will start to be drawn.
  6. Keep an eye on your opponent's progress bar because if they figure out the word correctly first, they will win the game.
  7. The first person to guess the word correctly or the last person standing wins!
  8. If it seems like the page has crashed, press the "reset" button in the upper left corner.
